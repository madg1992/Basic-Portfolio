About Me

I am a Front End developer with industry experience building websites and web applications. I specialize in JavaScript and have professional experience working with C# and Angular. I also have experience working with Vue, Ruby on Rails, and React. My role is to write and style the front-end components that meet the requirements of our mocks and fulfill our user stories. I also monitor and process pull requests for production deployments.

Technologies used: Angular, JavaScript, ES6, TypeScript, SASS

I like to solve problems. Throughout my career, I have been driven by my intellectual curiosity to find answers to the most pressing questions. Whether it's finding a perfect song for a film or finding a rare product for a rocket, I've been able to quickly uncover a customer's pain point and identify a strategic solution.

In my spare time, I enjoy jamming out with my band and creating covers of our favorite songs. Music has always played a very big part of my life. I started with the piano in the 3rd grade, played violin in 6th grade, and started singing at church when I was in 9th grade. Until now, I still maintain my love and passion for music. I'm a true believer in the popular saying, 'music feeds the soul'!

I decided to attend SMU’s Coding Bootcamp primarily because I wanted a career change. I acquired my Bachelor’s in Business Marketing but unfortunately I was not able to acquire the jobs that I really wanted. Coding, I think is a great fit for me because I was always told that I have a very creative mind. I delight in making my ideas come to life. The thought of designing my own web page or app and making it visually appealing through the front-end tools while also making it user friendly and intuitive using back-end programming languages really excites me.
